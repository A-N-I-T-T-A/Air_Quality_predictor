Air Quality Prediction

This Air Quality Prediction System uses machine learning to estimate pollution levels in real time. It analyzes factors like traffic, temperature, humidity, and industrial emissions to provide accurate insights.
With two models—Multi-Feature Predictor for detailed analysis and Single-Feature Predictor for quick assessments—the system helps individuals and policymakers make informed decisions about air quality.

Multi-Feature Predictor

This model utilizes multiple environmental factors such as traffic levels, temperature, humidity, and factory emissions to provide a more accurate air quality prediction. By considering various contributing elements, it offers a holistic view of air pollution trends.
